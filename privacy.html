Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Rewise. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address, name, and profile information (via Google Sign-in or direct registration).

Study Data: To make our spaced-repetition AI work, we collect the topics you add, your review history, study times, and retention scores. This is necessary for the app's core functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide, operate, and improve the Rewise app. Specifically, your study data is processed by our AI algorithms to generate optimal review schedules and personalized study materials just for you.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard backend infrastructure (Supabase). We enforce Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure that only you can access your personal study data. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to access, export, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account directly from within the Rewise app via Settings > Delete Account. Doing so will permanently erase all your personal data, topics, and review history from our servers.
