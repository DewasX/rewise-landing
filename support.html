Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rewise completely free? ▼

Yes. During our exclusive closed testing phase, Rewise gives you unrestricted access perfectly free of charge.

Why am I required to join a Google Group? ▼

Google Play Store policies require that apps in a "Closed Testing" phase strictly manage their tester lists using Google Groups. By joining with 2 clicks, you bypass the restriction automatically.

How does the AI Engine work? ▼

Our proprietary spaced-repetition AI instantly analyzes your inputs and generates tailored practice questions on the fly, saving you from manually writing flashcards.
