How to use Rewise

1

Add a Topic

Start by adding a subject or topic you need to study. You can set the estimated study time and difficulty.

2

Complete your first study session. Rewise will automatically schedule the next optimal time for you to review it based on spaced repetition algorithms.

3

Review & Master

Check your Dashboard daily. Rewise tells you exactly what to review and when, automatically adjusting if you forget something.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rewise completely free?

During our early access and closed testing phase, Rewise is completely free for all testers.

Why do I need to join a Google Group?

Google Play policies require apps in "Closed Testing" to manage tester access via Google Groups. Once you join the group, you get permission to download the app.

How does the AI work?

Rewise uses advanced AI to process your study topics and instantly generate practice questions and dynamic summaries, saving you hours of prep time.
